Position Description
You will be working with fellow software engineers focused on building out Tesla's internal tools that facilitate process management of the design, permitting, and installation of energy products and systems. This team is focused on building a stable and scalable platform that can be extended and configured for all Tesla Energy and Vehicle products that are sold and supported today, and any that will be introduced in the future. The goal of this role and team is to enable our internal employees with ways to track, standardize, automate, and ultimately optimize all steps taken on an energy installation job from start to finish.
